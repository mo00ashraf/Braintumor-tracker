Brain tumor detection

Brain tumor detection utilizes deep learning algorithms to automatically detect the presence of tumors or other abnormalities in MR images of the brain. By simply uploading an MR image, users can receive information about potential brain tumors or lesions without the need for a laborious diagnostic process.

Brain Tumor segmentation

Deep learning automates brain tumor segmentation in MRI images, providing precise tumor boundary information. Users upload MRI images to obtain detailed data for treatment planning and monitoring. This approach improves efficiency, accuracy, and eliminates manual analysis.
